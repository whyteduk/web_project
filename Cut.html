Cutting

  • What is cutting?

    What is cutting? You may have heard this term at the gym. But, what does it mean? Cutting is nothing more than lowering your fat percentage by adhering to a strict diet plan. This makes your muscle mass more visible. The difference between cutting and a regular diet is that with a regular diet you often lose muscle mass. Cutting is adjusting your diet so that the loss of muscle mass is minimal. If you still aren't clear on what it is, please click the read more below.
