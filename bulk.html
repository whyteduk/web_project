Bulking

  • What is bulking?

    So what exactly is “bulking”? If you aren’t aware, to build muscle you need to provide your body with excess calories. Everything in life is about energy. To add weight (be that fat or muscle) to your body you must supply the body with more energy (as calories from food) than it needs. We all need different amounts of calories. You can roughly work out your needs as they are dependent on you age, weight, height and activity; which is dependent on your job and how much you train. As long as you ingest more calories than you need, to maintain weight or referred to as maintenance calories, you’ll be in an energy surplus needed to gain weight.
